About LCBVC LLC

LCBVC LLC is a coffee shop store concept rooted in quality, craftsmanship, and community. Our approach celebrates carefully sourced beans, thoughtful roasting profiles, and a gold-and-black identity that reflects our commitment to elegance and warmth.

From slow mornings to late-night creativity, our future offerings will include signature roasts, seasonal blends, and brew guides—designed to help you make a perfect cup anywhere.
